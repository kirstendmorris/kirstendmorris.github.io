Coding Theory

My current research activity involves analyzing the performance of graph-based iterative decoding algorithms for Quantum Low Distance Parity Check codes. In order to do this I am working to improve my knowledge of discrete math, graph theory, coding (to simulate decoding algorithms), and quantum information science more broadly.
