Rethinking Number Theory

I am currently participating in the Rethinking Number Theory program, an AIM research community. This program is focused on fostering a joyful, affirming research experience for all participants, and I am loving it!! The image to the left is our logo for our team, the Coding Carrier Pigeons. This image was generated from AI from the text phrase "pigeon surrounded by mathematics" via the website Nightcafe.

  • Learn more about Rethinking Number Theory

Enhancing Diversity for Graduate Education (EDGE)

In the summer of 2016 I participated in the wonderful EDGE program. This is a 4-week intensive mathematical experiences that provides a crash-course on graduate-level mathematics and fosters a community and network to support women pursuing Ph.Ds in the mathematical sciences. I am deeply grateful for the EDGE community.

  • Learn more about EDGE

Research Experience for Undergraduates

In the summer of 2015 I participated in the California State University Channel Islands Research Experience for Undergraduates. This was my first time experiencing research mathematics, and I loved it!! It helped to have a wonderful project leader and project collaborators, many of whom I am still in close contact with today.
